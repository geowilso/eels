Coastal Memories Fading & Wistfulness of Magnetic Bird Song.

Michael Ridge

Composed from extended cassette tape loops of lo-fi ambient music and field recordings of bird song, chimes, streams/rivers and the coast. A soundtrack to fading coastal memories from long ago. Originally released on Quagga Curious Sounds as the fifth instalment in the Misc Loops series via a limited-edition cassette tape and digital download.
